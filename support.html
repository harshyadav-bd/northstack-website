Frequently Asked Questions

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data from our servers, including your profile, saved journeys, reminders, and notification preferences. This action cannot be undone. If you have an active subscription, remember to cancel it separately through your device's subscription settings to avoid continued billing.

On iPhone: Go to Settings > your name > Subscriptions > GOPulse > Cancel Subscription.

On Android: Open Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > GOPulse > Cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. For refund requests, please contact Apple or Google directly through their respective support channels.

Real-time train positions come from the Metrolinx Open Data API. Occasionally, the data feed may be delayed or temporarily unavailable. If positions aren't updating, it's likely an issue with the upstream data source rather than the app. Try pulling to refresh, or check back in a few minutes. If the issue persists, let us know.

Save your regular journey (e.g., Oakville to Union Station), then set a reminder with your desired arrival time and which days you commute. GOPulse will send you a notification with enough time to get to the station before your train departs. You can customize the buffer time and manage all your reminders from the app.

GOPulse caches station data and schedules locally, so you can still view saved journeys and recent schedule information when you're offline. However, real-time features like live train tracking, service alerts, and up-to-the-minute departure times require an internet connection. The app will clearly indicate when data is stale.

We collect only what's needed to run the app: your email for authentication, saved journeys and reminders, and anonymized crash data. We don't track you across apps, we don't show ads, and we don't sell your data. Location data is processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

We appreciate bug reports! You can use the feedback button in the app (Settings > Send Feedback), or email us directly at gopulse@northstacklabs.ca. If possible, include what you were doing when the issue occurred, your device model, and your OS version. Screenshots help too.
