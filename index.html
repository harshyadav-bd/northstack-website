About Us

Small team.
Real problems.

NorthStack started because we kept running into the same thing — apps that looked pretty but didn't actually work well for the people using them. We figured we could do better.

We're a small operation based in Ontario, Canada, and we like it that way. It means every project gets our full attention. We're not trying to be the next big agency. We just want to build software that people genuinely find useful.

Our first product, GOPulse, came out of a daily frustration — standing on a GO Transit platform wondering if the train was on time or if we'd be stuck waiting. Turns out a lot of other commuters felt the same way.

GOPulse Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

We built the features we wished we had as daily GO Transit riders.

Real-Time Tracking

See exactly where your train is on a live map. No more guessing if it's running late or if you have time to grab a coffee.

Smart Dashboard

Your home screen shows departures from your nearest station. Save your regular journeys and see live times instantly.

Departure Reminders

Tell us when you need to arrive, and we'll nudge you when it's time to head to the station. Never miss your train again.

Service Alerts

Delays, cancellations, platform changes — know about disruptions before you're standing on the platform.

Journey Planner

Plan trips across the network, including ones with transfers. Save your regular routes for quick access every day.

Station Details

Parking availability, facilities, accessibility info, and platform details for every GO station in the network.
