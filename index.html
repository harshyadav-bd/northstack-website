About

One person.
Real problems.

Hey, I'm Harsh. A software engineer based in Ontario, Canada who cares deeply about building things that actually work. Well-designed, scalable software that solves real problems for real people. That's what gets me out of bed.

NorthStack started with a simple belief: the best software comes from people who feel the problem themselves. I wanted to build things I'd use with my own hands, not just ship features into a void. GOPulse, my first product, came from exactly that. I ride the GO Train every weekday and most weekends. The frustration was personal, so the solution had to be too.

I care about craft. Good software isn't just functional, it's thoughtful. It respects your time, works when you need it, and stays out of your way when you don't. Whether it's a transit app or whatever comes next, that's the standard.

01

Use What You Build

I don't ship things I wouldn't use myself. If I'm not reaching for it daily, it's not ready.

02

Design Is Not Optional

Product

Meet GOPulse

Real-time train tracking, smart reminders, and service alerts for GO Transit riders across Ontario.

GOPulse
Your commute,
simplified.

Built by a GO commuter, for GO commuters. Track your train in real time, plan journeys with transfers, and get notified before your train leaves so you never have to wonder "is it on time?" again.

Available soon on App Store & Google Play
GOPulse Features

Built for the
daily commute.

The features we wished we had as daily GO Transit riders.

Real-Time Tracking

See exactly where your train is on a live map. No more guessing if it's running late or if you have time to grab a coffee.

Smart Dashboard

Your home screen shows departures from your nearest station. Save your regular journeys and see live times instantly.

Departure Reminders

Set your arrival time and get notified when it's time to head to the station. No more missed trains.

Service Alerts

Delays, cancellations, platform changes. Know about disruptions before you leave the house.

Journey Planner

Plan trips across the network, including ones with transfers. Save your regular routes for quick access every day.

Station Details

Parking availability, facilities, accessibility info, and platform details for every GO station in the network.
